About Me

My name is Matt Wigdahl and I'm 38 years old. I'm from Salt Lake City, Utah. I have two fun-lovin' daugthers and a beautiful (and supportive!) wife, Jane.

My favorite hobbies are basically anything that has to do with the outdoors. I love to ski, bike, raft, flyfish and hike.
